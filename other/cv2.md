## Developed Scientific Software

- **Magnes (2017–Present):** GPU-accelerated simulator of magnetic systems with a focus on lattice/atomistic models, efficient computation of metastable states, minimum energy paths, and transition rates using harmonic transition state theory. Scalable to millions of degrees of freedom.\
  [https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/magnes](https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/magnes)

- **LCSim (2021–Present):** Simulator for photo-responsive liquid crystals based on Oseen-Frank theory with director field representation. Includes optical simulation via Jones calculus.\
  [https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/lcsim](https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/lcsim)

- **Topovec (2024–Present):** Visualization tool for 3D vector fields (e.g., director or magnetization fields), building on concepts from Magnes and LCSim.\
  [https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/topovec](https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/topovec)
