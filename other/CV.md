# Developed scientific software

* **Magnes** 2017- Magnetic systems simulator with primary focus on lattice/atomistic models, fast computation of metastable states, minimum energy paths and transition rate in the framework of harmonic transition rate theory. The code is GPU accelerated and capable of working with millions of degrees of freedom. [https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/magnes](https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/magnes)   
* **LCSim** 2021- Simulator of photo-active liquid crystal using Oseen-Frank energy and director order parameter. Also simulates optics using Jones calculus. [https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/lcsim](https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/lcsim)    
* **Topovec** 2024- Visualization of 3d vector fields, such as director field in liquid crystals or magnetization field in magnets. The package extends approaches developed for Magnes and LCSim. [https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/topovec](https://gitlab.com/alepoydes/topovec)
